about arc hub factory

A focused profile page for ARC as a high-end bicycle hub manufacturer and custom hub supplier.

company profile

professional hub manufacturing

ARC Hub Factory was established in 2013 and is located in Taicang, Jiangsu Province. The company focuses on design, research and development, manufacturing, sales and customization of high-end hubs for bikes and e-bikes.

Its website content positions the company as a professional factory for high-end bicycle hubs, combining product development, production, quality control, sales and personalized custom service.
